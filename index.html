Thomas A. Edison Career and Technical Education High School, located in Queens, New York, is a renowned institution known for its dedication to providing students with comprehensive career and technical education. Founded in honor of the prolific inventor Thomas Edison, the school offers a diverse range of programs aimed at preparing students for success in various fields, including technology, engineering, health sciences, and more. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dynamic learning environment, Thomas A. Edison CTE High School emphasizes hands-on learning experiences, allowing students to develop practical skills and explore their interests. The school prides itself on its commitment to fostering innovation, creativity, and excellence, empowering students to thrive both academically and professionally in an ever-evolving world.
